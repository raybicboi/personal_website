Incentivized Activity Monitoring in Jamaica (IAMJ)

Technical Consultant

Diabetes in Jamaica is described as an epidemic as it is found in 12% of the population who are 15 years or older, and it is the leading cause of death of women in Jamaica (per Jamaica's Ministry of Health and Wellness). Habit formation with regards to increased physical activity is not only critical in addressing this problem but is a low-cost way for people with diabetes to make changes in their lifestyles.

Teaming up with two other student consultants, we were task to the University Hospital of West Indies's no-code software stack (Xano & AppGyver) to create a userface linked to a Postgres database that manages the hospital's diabetes patients.

ClearTerms

Front End Developer + More

According to the researchers behind the ClearTerms project, over 99% of users do not read past the first paragraph to a service's terms and conditions (T & C) statement before consenting to it. This project comprises of a machine learning model that reads terms and conditions and predicts a level of credibility of the statements based on previously used statements.

My role was to assist another developer in developing a website that succinctly describes the ClearTerms project and its findings. The site uses HTML, CSS, Javascript, and a touch of PHP. In addition, I also wrote a python script that searches the web for different companies for services and extracts their T & C contents.
